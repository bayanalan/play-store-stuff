PRIVACY POLICY

1. INTRODUCTION

Thank you for playing Flappy Buzz! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you play our mobile game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Local Game Data

Flappy Buzz stores the following information locally on your device:

  • High scores and best performance records
  • Unlocked character skins and customization preferences
  • Game settings (audio preferences, performance mode, language selection)
  • Achievement progress and unlocked achievements
  • Lifetime gameplay statistics (total games played, scores, shields collected)
  • Selected language preference

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

Flappy Buzz is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, advertising ID)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking
  • Crash reports or error logs
  • Any information transmitted to external servers
  • Camera, microphone, or contact access

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

3.1 Local Game Functions

All data stored on your device is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress and high scores
  • Remember your preferred game settings
  • Track achievement progress and unlock rewards
  • Maintain lifetime statistics for your gaming experience
  • Preserve your customization choices (skins, language, audio)

3.2 No External Data Processing

Your game data remains on your device and is never:

  • Transmitted to our servers or third parties
  • Used for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Shared with analytics services
  • Processed for behavioral analysis

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 Local Storage Only

All game data is stored locally on your device using standard mobile app storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device.

4.2 Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Reset all game data using the in-game reset feature (Alt+J keyboard shortcut)
  • Clear data by uninstalling the app
  • Modify settings at any time through the game interface

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

Flappy Buzz does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or external APIs. The game operates entirely offline except for font loading from Google Fonts for display purposes.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Flappy Buzz is suitable for players of all ages, including children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no additional privacy concerns for younger players. Parents can be confident that no personal data is collected from their children.

7. INTERNATIONAL USERS

Since all data remains on your device and no information is transmitted externally, there are no cross-border data transfer concerns. The game operates the same way regardless of your location.

8. YOUR RIGHTS

8.1 Data Access

You can view your game data through the in-game statistics and achievements screens.

8.2 Data Deletion

You can delete all game data at any time by:

  • Using the in-game reset feature
  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app

8.3 Data Portability

Game data is stored locally on your device. While the game does not include export features, all data remains accessible through the game interface and is permanently tied to your device installation.

9. MODDING AND CUSTOMIZATION

Flappy Buzz supports user modifications (mods) that run locally on your device. These mods:

  • Execute only on your device
  • Cannot access external networks
  • Cannot collect or transmit personal information
  • Are sandboxed within the game environment

10. GOOGLE PLAY STORE COMPLIANCE

This privacy policy complies with Google Play Store requirements:

  • Full disclosure of data collection practices (none in our case)
  • Transparent handling of user information
  • Appropriate privacy protections for all age groups
  • No undisclosed data sharing or collection

11. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Since the game does not collect personal data, such changes would primarily be clarifications or additions to better explain our privacy-first approach.

Updated policies will be posted at this location and, if significant, communicated through app store update descriptions.

12. EFFECTIVE POLICY

This Privacy Policy is effective immediately and applies to all versions of Flappy Buzz. Our commitment to user privacy has been consistent since the game's initial release.
